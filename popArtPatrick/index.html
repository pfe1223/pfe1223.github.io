Claes Oldenburg

Claes is known for his commically large sculptures of ordinary objects . For example, he made 45-foot-tall clothespin for the city of Philadelphia.

X

Cupid's Span

The 60-foot-tall bow and arrow on the Embarcadero is the work of Oldenburg. The sculpture was trucked in overnight and assembled by dawn.

X

3D Pop Art

Prior to Oldenburg, Pop artists worked with 2D mediums. His squishy textures and outlandish sizes brought sculpture into the realm of Pop.

X

Subtle Critique

Oldenburg didn't use the mechanized or comicbook approach of his peers. He shared their same ideas and values, but Oldenburg's use of size and texture provided a gentler critique.
